Details about the Rice Classifier

This rice classifier uses a deep learning model to classify rice types based on images. The model was trained on several types of rice to predict which variety the image represents.

  • Arborio: Known for its use in risottos.
  • Basmati: A fragrant long-grain rice often used in Indian cuisine.
  • Ipsala: A variety of rice grown in Turkey.
  • Jasmine: A fragrant rice variety popular in Southeast Asia.
  • Karacadag: A Turkish rice variety.
